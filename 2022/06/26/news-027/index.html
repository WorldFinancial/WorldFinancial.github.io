What can you get for a $128 million Warren Buffett lunch?

Created2022-06-26|Updated2023-08-03|Internet
|Post View:

Recently, the much-anticipated “Warren Buffett Lunch” finally settled on June 18.

The final price of this “Buffett Lunch Auction” was $19.01 million, or about RMB 128 million, which set a new record.

Some people quipped, “Lunch is easy to eat, but the essence is hard to learn”. As Warren Buffett’s last lunch, a total of five bidders participated, after 43 rounds of bidding, before the final result was finalized. It is clear that this lunch is not easy to eat, and the essence is not easy to master.

The inspiration for the lunch
Warren Buffett, the former richest man in the world, started buying stocks at the age of 11 and was sought after by investors all over the world for his unique vision of the capital market. It is believed that this wise man, who is sitting on a huge fortune, has mastered the secret key to open the door to success.

With him at the helm of Berkshire Hathaway Inc. According to Forbes magazine, Buffett’s net worth as of June 18 was approximately $93.4 billion, still ranking seventh in the world, and his total earnings increased more than 24,700 times in the 55 years before the epidemic.

Buffett’s experience and stature have made him famous. At the same time, they are aware of this value and are committed to maximizing its effect.

Buffett’s lunch auction was inspired by his first wife, Susan. Susan is a very passionate philanthropist and had a long history of working closely with the Glide Foundation. To show his wife’s support, Buffett put up an old wallet that he had used for years for auction, eventually fetching $210,000. Later, he decided that auctioning off the item was meaningless because no material thing meant anything to him, and he decided that time was what was most valuable, so the auction object was upgraded to a lunchtime companionship. The successful bidder gets to have a lunch with him at Smith & Wollensky’s Steakhouse in Manhattan, New York.

This auction event started in 2000 and so far, 22 years have passed. The lowest winning bid in this event was in 2001, when it cost only $18,000. The proceeds from each charity auction are donated to the Glad Foundation, a San Francisco-based charity that helps the local poor and homeless.

Buffett’s lunch, a prop for speculators
For most people, a lunch that costs very little and repeatedly fetches sky-high prices may be a bit unbelievable. But for Warren Buffett’s pilgrims, the investment in the lunch was well worth it.

In previous auctions, there have been dozens of celebrities who have had lunch with Warren Buffett. But most of these people have their own purposes, partly to pay their respects to their idol, but also partly to market themselves. According to statistics, four of the lunch with Warren Buffett came from China, namely Duan Yongping, Zhao Danyang, Zhu Ye and Sun Yuchen.

In 2006, Duan Yongping won the auction for $620,000, making him the first person in China to win a lunch with Warren Buffett, as well as the founder of the “Little King” learning machine and the “BBK” brand. Duan Yongping said he participated in the auction to pay tribute to his investment icon, saying, “This is not a business, I’m not trying to ask him for advice, and he doesn’t have any secrets to tell me. What he can tell us, he has already told us in public. I can pay more attention to his investment philosophy by having a meal with him, so I can make fewer mistakes in the future.

Secondly, in 2008, the “Godfather of Private Equity” Zhao Danyang won the bid with $2.11 million and won the lunch qualification. It is reported that during the lunch, Zhao Danyang recommended the stock to Warren Buffett, and eventually the stock rose 24% in four days, Zhao Danyang scored more than 100 million. After this lunch, Warren Buffett made a rule of “no talking about individual stocks at lunch”.

Zhu Ye, the third person in China to have lunch with Warren Buffett, took the lunch in 2015 for $2.35 million, which was a big expense for Tianshin Entertainment at the time. He introduced his company to Warren Buffett at the meeting, and as a result, the stock price rose from 66 yuan at the time to 125 yuan, and the market value once exceeded 35 billion yuan. But the wind came and went quickly. 2018 saw a succession of financial problems, including debt defaults and huge losses, for Tianshen Entertainment. In May of the same year, Zhu Ye was investigated by the Securities Regulatory Commission for allegedly violating securities laws and regulations.

The last one was Sun Yuchen, founder of Wavefield TRON, who qualified for a Warren Buffett lunch in 2019 with $4.568 million, the highest record at the time. However, after the deal, Sun Yuchen let Warren Buffett off the hook once. Claiming that the meeting with Warren Buffett was cancelled for medical reasons, it was postponed to January 2020. According to related reports, Sun Yuchen gave Buffett two Samsung Galaxy Fold folding screen phones with 1 bitcoin built in, as well as 1930830 BoF coins representing Buffett’s birth date, but these were donated to the Glade Foundation by Buffett.

Warren Buffett has been known to loathe bitcoin, describing it as a “crook’s tool” and “rat poison squared. So far, his rejection of bitcoin remains unchanged.

The last “lunch” is coming to an end
It is understood that this session of the Buffett lunch will be the last public auction charity event. Buffett himself has said that he will not be participating in the lunch auction, but he believes that the Berkshire Hathaway tradition of lunch auctions will continue. Future events may be a continuation of Buffett’s three children, especially Berkshire board members Howard Buffett and Susan Buffett.

The Glide Foundation also said in a statement, “Mr. Buffett’s friendship and generosity are priceless. Our precious friendship will continue, but 2022 will be the final song of this special event.”

Since its inception, the Buffett’s Lunch program has raised more than $53 million to support GLIDE’s innovative philanthropic services.

Although the Buffett Lunch auction has been conducted in the name of charity for many years and has gained worldwide attention, the charity is only part of the event; it is the value of Buffett himself that attracts people to bid. In a classic phrase, “It doesn’t matter what you eat, it’s who you eat with”, and only you know how much you can “eat”.
