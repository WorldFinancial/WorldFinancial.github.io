Huobi Global Launches Third PrimeEarn Event with US$230 Million in Assets for Deposit

Created2022-03-20|Updated2023-08-03|coin
|Post View:

One of the world’s leading digital asset exchanges, Huobi Global announced its third PrimeEarn event, aimed to help their users gain stable profits in the turmoil market. The event was launched after the PrimeEarn EveryDay event held in this month, and the deposit has exceeded 300 million US dollars at the end of the event.

PrimeEarn is a fixed deposit product that allows investors to stake their assets for set periods, as short as a week or as long as a month, in exchange for a return amount. An easy and relatively risk-free way to get started with investing, products like PrimeEarn are gaining traction in the crypto community by incentivizing fixed deposit investors with high Annual Percentage Yield (APY). Given how many financial management products in the market are flexible deposit products with low APY, PrimeEarn events have proven popular among investors since PrimeEarn’s launch at the end of 2021.

PrimeEarn’s third and latest event will reward investors with higher APYs and will include the following:

Deposit for main assets and popular assets: USDT, BTC, ETH, HUSD, UST, SOL and MATIC.

APY ranging from 5% to 35%.

Staking period: 14 days

Maximum bet limit of US $230 million

No individual cap

The 14-day APYs for staking assets such as SOL and MATIC can reach as high as 35% — nearly twice the yields of competing products in the market.

“The products from the first two campaigns were sold out in about 4 days, so we decided to increase the frequency of holding PrimeEarn events in order to cater more market needs,” said Jeff Mei, Director of Global Strategy. “In addition to APYs which can go as high as 35%, we’re giving out 50,000 pieces of 100% APY booster coupons this time, which can add significantly to the base APY. I believe our users will be more than satisfied with their rewards.”

Starting at 11:00 (UTC) on Feb 23 2022, users will be able to wager assets on PrimeEarn in exchange for APY of up to 35%.
