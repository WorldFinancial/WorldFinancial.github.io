Residents in the vicinity of the late-night rights of BYD factory and into big trouble

Created2022-05-09|Updated2023-08-03|Internet
|Post View:

Just now, Changsha Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government have set up an investigation team and organized relevant experts to station at the factory to launch an investigation. According to several media reports, recently, Changsha BYD Yuhua District factory gate gathered a hundred nearby residents late at night pulling banners to defend their rights, strongly demanding BYD to solve the pollution problem. It is reported that the BYD factory affects more than ten nearby communities, some less than 100 meters away, and the nearest is even only 50 meters away.

It is worth noting that at the end of April, this Changsha factory of BYD had three employees jumping from buildings within a week. According to Red Star Capital Bureau, among the news circulating within the employees, one of them jumped because of emotional problems, another because of gambling, and the others were not clear.

As early as April, some residents reflected online the pungent smell emanating from the Changsha BYD factory, and during the same period, some owners and children experienced nausea and vomiting, coughing, nosebleeds and other physical symptoms.

On May 7, the topic of “BYD factory pollution suspected to cause a number of children nosebleeds” was on the hot search.
On the evening of May 7, BYD’s official Weibo issued a statement saying that the BYD Changsha Yuhua District Industrial Park was put into operation in 2012, and the discharge of the park complies with relevant national regulations and standards. In some residential areas close to the industrial park, there may be odors, and the company has taken relevant measures and actively improved them. Regarding the situation of “excessive discharge causing nosebleeds” on the Internet, it is a maliciously fabricated connection. We have called the police and will investigate the legal responsibility of the relevant personnel.

In an online recording, the general manager of BYD’s Changsha region said that the factory had invested more than 10 billion before and after, and it was unrealistic to stop production.

According to the Changsha Evening News, on May 8, in response to the public’s feedback on the gas emissions of BYD Yuhua District’s factories, the Changsha Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government have established an investigation team to organize government functional departments, third-party testing agencies and relevant experts to enter BYD Yuhua. District factories to investigate.

One wave has not settled, and another has risen. Following the incident of jumping off the building, BYD has encountered new troubles from the “pollution gate” this time. It is foreseeable that the impact on its stock price and the production capacity of new energy vehicles will be affected.

It is reported that BYD sold 105,475 passenger vehicles in April, a year-on-year increase of 313.22%. As of the close on May 5, BYD reported 242.08 yuan, down 2.7%, with a total market value of 704.7 billion yuan.

The factory was built first and then the community was built. The factory involved has been ordered to rectify many times.

It is understood that Changsha BYD Automobile Co., Ltd. was established in August 2009 with a registered capital of 1 billion yuan. Changsha BYD Yuhua District Factory is the first project of BYD Group in the central region. It has 17,000 employees and a total investment of more than 10.7 billion yuan. Energy vehicle strategic base.

BYD faces investigation, production capacity may be greatly affected

In early May, BYD’s April production and sales report showed that new energy vehicles sold 106,042 units in April, compared with 25,662 units in the same period last year; the cumulative sales of new energy vehicles from January to April were 392,371 units, a year-on-year increase of 387.94%.

BYD produced 107,478 new energy vehicles in April, compared with 27,115 in the same period last year; the cumulative output of new energy vehicles from January to April was 395,008, a year-on-year increase of 377.44%.

BYD’s factory in Yuhua District is known as the “largest production capacity”. It can be speculated that behind this excellent production capacity in April, the factory is producing day by day.

According to Phoenix.com, the first phase of BYD’s Yuhua District factory will start construction in January 2021, and it will be completed and put into operation in July. The second phase of the project has also completed the construction of some workshops and workshops. It is expected that the project will be officially put into production in May this year. After the project is fully put into use, Changsha BYD has become the production base with the largest number of vehicle products and the largest production capacity, with an annual output of more than 600,000 new energy vehicles.
