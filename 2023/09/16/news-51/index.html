The central bank exceeded the renewal of 591 billion yuan MLF! MLF plus the renewal of stacked downgrade reflects the determination to stabilize growth!

Created2023-09-16|Updated2023-09-16|Internet
|Post View:

Experts: do not completely rule out the possibility of continuing to lower the MLF operating rate

Caixin News Agency, September 15 (Reporter Shi Si-Tong) in the context of last month’s interest rate cut, today’s cut of 0.25 percentage points, the central bank sent out another message, said that in order to maintain a reasonable abundance of liquidity in the banking system, to maintain the end-of-quarter liquidity stable, the People’s Bank of China carried out a 591 billion yuan medium-term lending facilitation (MLF) operations, the winning interest rate of 2.50% to remain unchanged on the same day to carry out a 139 billion yuan Open market reverse repurchase operation.

In the view of industry insiders, the MLF increase in September rose directly due to the recent tightening of market liquidity, the bank’s demand for MLF operations increased. And MLF plus renewed superimposed on the landing of the cut, the release of support for banks to continue to increase credit investment, consolidate the economic upturn upward policy signals, is expected to drive the new credit in September, the continuation of the momentum of social financing rose sharply.

As for whether the future will continue to cut interest rates, lower MLF operating rate, a number of institutions expect to cut interest rates during the year there is still room, while there are also voices that the current domestic policy space is still sufficient, but short-term once again to cut the threshold of interest rate cuts lifted. “This mainly depends on the macroeconomic and property market direction before the end of the year, can not completely exclude this possibility.” Some industry insiders said.

Banking system liquidity tightened, MLF operation demand rose

“The rise in MLF additions in September is directly attributable to the recent tightening of market liquidity and the increased demand for MLF operations by banks.”

Wang Qing, chief macro analyst at Dongfang Jincheng, pointed out that in the previous four months, due to the market interest rate continued to be lower than the corresponding policy rate, the medium- and long-term liquidity in the banking system is abundant, resulting in low demand for MLF operations by commercial banks. Recently, by the credit investment significantly increased, special bond issuance is experiencing the highest peak of the year and other factors, since September market interest rates have risen sharply, in which 1-year commercial banks (AAA grade) interbank certificates of deposit maturity yield has risen to the MLF operating rate near the DR007 is to continue to run in the short-term policy rate (the central bank’s 7-day reverse repurchase rate) above. This means that the banking system liquidity is tightening, MLF operation demand rises accordingly.

It is worth noting that on the evening of September 14 the central bank just announced the implementation of a 0.25 percentage point cut, which is expected to inject more than 500 billion liquidity into the banking system.

“In this context, today’s MLF is still a large-scale addition to the renewal, showing that the policy surface clearly supports the broad credit process to continue to speed up, is expected to drive the new credit in September, social financing to continue the momentum of a substantial rebound.” Wang Qing believes that the MLF plus volume operation and the reduction can supplement the banking system in the medium and long term liquidity, the latter can also reduce the cost of bank funds, the release of a more obvious signal of stable growth.

He further analyzed that the bank is currently adjusting the stock of mortgage interest rates, the reduction in the combination of deposit interest rates down, can to a large extent to ease the pressure of narrowing the bank’s net interest rate spread, help banks to maintain sound operation, to prevent financial risks. At the same time, more importantly, the cut also released a clear signal of counter-cyclical regulation encoded, superimposed on the renewal of today’s MLF addition, helping to further guide social expectations, boost market confidence, consolidate the foundation of the economic upturn to the good.

“From past experience, the restart of the 14-day reverse repurchase is mainly to cope with the end of the season short-term factors on the capital surface disturbance, to meet the short-term funding needs of institutions.” Zhou Maohua, a macro researcher at Everbright Bank’s Financial Markets Department, believes that the central bank’s MLF price leveling and volume increase operation after the overnight rate cut, the total amount (quasi-decrease) and the structural tools with the increase in the real economy to support the strength of the central bank’s determination to stabilize growth is fully embodied.

In his view, the quasi-reduction + MLF plus volume renewal, on the one hand, the release of long term liquidity, helping banks and financial institutions to optimize the funding structure, stabilize the cost of liabilities, and enhance the ability of credit injection; on the one hand, to better match the implementation of the positive fiscal policy, and to meet the demand for inter-seasonal funds of the institution; at the same time, the central bank policy continues to be forceful, to further boost the confidence of the market, and to promote the accelerated balance of the economy. “The central bank policy portfolio also reflects the central bank in stabilizing the total volume at the same time, pay more attention to the policy precision quality effect. Maintain the total volume of steady growth, while guiding financial institutions to increase the weak links in the real economy, the key emerging areas of support.”

Will the future continue to cut interest rates and lower the MLF operating rate?

Talking about whether the future will continue to cut interest rates and lower the MLF operating rate, Wang Qing said that this mainly depends on the macroeconomic and property market direction before the end of the year, and can not be completely ruled out this possibility.

“Taking into account the next period of time domestic prices will continue to be at a low level, but also for further downward policy interest rates to provide more favorable conditions. We believe that the regulatory layer will mainly use the exchange rate stabilization policy tools to deal with RMB exchange rate fluctuations, and the risk of the currency market can be controlled.”

In Wang Qing’s view, the exchange rate factor will not pose a substantial obstacle to the flexible adjustment of domestic monetary policy. Despite the recent depreciation of the RMB, the central bank still cut the MLF operating rate in June and August; and this cut also confirms once again that the central bank will maintain the independence of monetary policy in the first place.

In fact, yesterday evening, the central bank announced the second cut in the year, there are a number of institutions expect to cut interest rates within the year there is still room. Among them, Western Securities pointed out that if the real estate market demand continues to be low, the year does not rule out the possibility of further cuts in interest rates. At the same time, Zheshang Securities is expected to monetary policy within the year, there are still interest rate cuts, continue to drive down the cost of corporate financing, the point in the fourth quarter of the probability of greater.

And in the view of Zhou Maohua, the current domestic policy space is still sufficient, but the short-term again cut rates and interest rate threshold raised. “The main thing is that the economy is stabilizing and improving trend, the central bank after another, the policy effect is gradually released, the need to maintain a certain degree of patience and determination, at the same time, excessive easing of the policy may have potential risks.”

According to Zhou Maohua’s analysis, the recently released data show that the effect of domestic macro policy has been revealed, the demand repair momentum has been enhanced, and the market is expected to gradually improve. From the policy time lag point of view, the domestic fiscal and taxation, monetary policy, stabilize the property market, promote investment and other policy combinations effect will be further released, with the confidence rebound, the effect of these policies will be more obvious.

From the MLF tool positioning, this incremental renewal, moderately increase the medium and long term limited capital investment, is to continue to guide the financial institutions to increase the support for small and micro enterprises and other weak links in the real economy, manufacturing and other key emerging areas, and to release a positive signal of stable growth.

He believes that this month’s LPR rate will remain unchanged, mainly since June, the central bank exceeded expectations to implement two interest rate cuts, is currently in the process of promoting the implementation of the specific landing of financial institutions, especially in the mortgage interest rate, to promote local policy in accordance with the city, use the policy space, promote policies to speed up the landing of the effect; part of the banks to reduce the interest rate differential pressure is still large; at the same time, the need to guard against the potential risks of excessive monetary policy easing, and take into account both internal and external Balanced.
